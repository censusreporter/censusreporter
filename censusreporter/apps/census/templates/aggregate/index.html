Aggregate ACS Data for a Custom Area

Draw a shape on the map (or upload a GeoJSON file), pick a Census table, and Census Reporter will total the American Community Survey estimates for every geography of the chosen level that overlaps your shape — correctly combining the margins of error. Statistics that can't be added across areas (medians, per-capita, etc.) are listed but not aggregated.

Use the polygon/rectangle tools at the top-left of the map to draw, or edit an existing shape.

Draw or upload a shape to begin.
